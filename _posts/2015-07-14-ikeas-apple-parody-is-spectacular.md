<p><span class="postStandFirst">IKEA has reached hilarious marketing heights with its impossibly apt Apple parody.</span></p>
<p><img class=" size-full wp-image-7339 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/IKEA-Apple-Parody.jpg" alt="<img src=https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/IKEA-Apple-Parody.jpg alt=&rdquo;IKEA&rsquo;s Apple Parody&rdquo;/>" width="854" height="469" /></p>
<p>It measures just 8mm thin and weighs less than 400g. There isn&rsquo;t a cable or wire in sight, yet it&rsquo;s fully powered with an eternally lasting battery.</p>
<p>Tactile touch technology allows you to navigate it, by swiping left-to-right to move forwards, and right-to-left to move backwards.</p>
<p>And best of all, it&rsquo;s pre-loaded with thousands of inspiring, home furnished ideas, so you can get started straight from the box.</p>
<p>No, this isn&rsquo;t Apple&rsquo;s latest tech delight; meet IKEA&rsquo;s 2015 catalogue &ndash; The BookBook.</p>
<p>IKEA&rsquo;s impish marketing gurus have produced a perfectly executed Apple parody, lampooning the brand that&rsquo;s famous for doting on its <a href="http://www.buymobiles.net/apple" target="_blank">iPhones</a>, MacBooks and pretty much everything else it creates.</p>
<p>The spoof nails Apple&rsquo;s marketing characteristics, from the minimalist backdrop, to Jonny Ive&rsquo;s grey tee, to the levitating products and uplifting background chimes.</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s spectacular.</p>
