<p><span class="postStandFirst">Apple-enamoured designers have been flexing their creative talent, with a series of beautiful iPhone 7 concepts.</span></p>
<p>Apple&rsquo;s ability to summon up a storm of intrigue and captivation is perhaps even more remarkable than the products it builds.</p>
<p>There simply isn&rsquo;t a gadget launch as eagerly anticipated as that of an iPhone&hellip;</p>
<p>And for many, the long wait in between Apple&rsquo;s annual keynotes is just too much to bear.</p>
<p>Thankfully, though, some creative individuals have been drafting concepts of what the iPhone 7 (iPhone 6s?) might look like, or at least what they&rsquo;d like it to look like.</p>
<p>So for any Apple addicts looking for a quick fix, please help yourself to these 10 beautiful iPhone 7 concepts.</p>
<h2>1.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7560 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-1.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (1)" width="800" height="450" data-id="7560" /><em>This iPhone looks like a sleek blend of Apple&rsquo;s own Magic Mouse and the curved LG G4.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">2.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7561 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-2.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (2)" width="800" height="800" data-id="7561" /><em>Dubbed iPhone Edition, this is reminiscent of the original iPhone, refreshed with more premium materials.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">3.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7563 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-3.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (3)" width="800" height="500" data-id="7563" /><em>Clearly inspired by Apple Watch, this iPhone 7 concept is adorned with a Digital Crown.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">4.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7564 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-4.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (4)" width="800" height="449" data-id="7564" /><em>I can&rsquo;t help but picture EVE from WALL-E every time I look at this.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">5.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7568 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-5.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (5)" width="640" height="442" data-id="7568" />Borrowing the design of Apple&rsquo;s iPod Nano, this concept looks beautifully slim and compact.</p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">6.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7569 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-6.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (6)" width="800" height="640" data-id="7569" /><em>This creation is rather rugged, with a return to sharper edges. The home button appears to have been replaced with a more industrial branding.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">7.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7570 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-7.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (7)" width="800" height="424" data-id="7570" /><em>Android meets iPhone in this consideration. Though very unlikely, it&rsquo;s a fun idea.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">8.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7571 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-8.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (8)" width="640" height="480" data-id="7571" /><em>Pushing the display to the very edges, this iPhone 7 concept works around the home button quite artfully.</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">9.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7572 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-9.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (9)" width="800" height="449" data-id="7572" /><em>Similar to iPhone 6, however the volume rocker has been replaced by touch-sensitive technology, the headphone jack has been removed all together, and the speakers have been relocated to the sides</em></p>
<h2 style="text-align: left;">10.</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class=" size-full wp-image-7573 aligncenter" src="https://a1comms-blog-buymobiles.storage.googleapis.com/2015/07/iPhone-7-concept-10.jpg" alt="iPhone 7 concept (10)" width="800" height="480" data-id="7573" /><em>Again, similar to iPhone 6, though the profile looks to have been trimmed down somewhat, and the edges are sharper than the chamfered ones we&rsquo;re used to.</em></p>
