<p><span class="postStandFirst" style="color: #0896d5; line-height: 26px; font-size: 18px;">Samsung Galaxy S series &ndash; one of the most iconic range of mobile phones ever to hit the shelves, have been providing us with premium devices for nearly a decade.</span></p>
<p><!-- /wp:paragraph --></p>
<p>Where has the time gone?!</p>
<p>With nearly 10 years of Galaxy S smartphone creations, we&rsquo;ve arrived in 2019 with the launch of Samsung&rsquo;s most impressive to date.</p>
<p>Samsung <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10-128gb-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Galaxy S10</a>, <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10-plus-128gb-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">S10 Plus</a> and <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10e-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">S10e</a> offer amazing smartphone screens, cameras and under-the-hood tech that warrants Samsung's place in the top 3 mobile phone brands.</p>
<p>To put things into perspective, and how big the Samsung brand is, worldwide, the South Korean tech giant shipped nearly 300 million* devices in 2018 alone. It&rsquo;s these kinds of numbers that plant Samsung among the global leaders.</p>
<p>To fully understand how far the Samsung Galaxy S devices have come, you have to start right at the beginning of the Galaxy journey, with the original Samsung Galaxy S.</p>

<h2>Samsung Galaxy S</h2>
<p>In March 2010, Samsung launched the first Galaxy S series device. At the time, it boasted best-in-class technology, including a powerful GPU, a 4-inch Super AMOLED display and a 5-megapixel camera. For enhanced usability, Galaxy S came with a fresh Quick Panel feature allowing easy transitions between apps.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20573" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/galaxy-s-edit.png" alt="" width="600" height="669"></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy SII</h2>
<p>Galaxy SII came with more enhancements to the original &lsquo;S&rsquo; version. In 2011, this device offered usability enhancements with a snazzy 4.3-inch Super AMOLED display, Android Gingerbread and a slimmer design.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20572" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/5292013115052PM_635_samsung_galaxy_s_II_plus-edit.png" alt="" width="600" height="449"></p>
<p><span class="" style="font-size: 23px; color: #444444;">Next: See Galaxy SIII and Galaxy S4.</span></p>
<p><!--nextpage--></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy SIII</h2>
<p>Samsung took things up another level with the Galaxy S3. In 2012 this handset had a rounder look and made for convenience with its handy day-to-day features. The front camera even recognised when you were looking at the screen, maintaining brightness levels for you. Sometimes it's the little things that make the difference.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20587" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/5292013114707PM_635_samsung_galaxy_s_III-edit.png" alt="" width="600" height="449"></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S4</h2>
<p>The Samsung Galaxy S4 was released with more enhanced usability features as well as better cameras and a bigger screen. Its rounded design was developed to house a 5-inch Full HD display. Galaxy S4 came with a dual camera setup that worked simultaneously letting you snap with both the front and rear camera.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20579" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/samsung-galaxy-s4-1-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="449"></p></p>
<p><span class="" style="font-size: 23px; color: #444444;">Next: See Galaxy S5 and Galaxy S6/S6 Edge.</span></p>
<p><!--nextpage--></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S5</h2>
<p>The advancements with Galaxy S5 are certainly starting to be more in-line with smartphone tech we expect on modern devices. Samsung took a leap and released the first smartphone with a built-in heart rate monitor, with a strong health and fitness focus. Not only that, it was made with dust and water protection in mind.</p>
<p>There were advancements in other areas to with Galaxy S5, a slightly larger screen, more battery power, and more camera power.</p>
<h2><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20578" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/Samsung-Galaxy-S5-Charcoal-Black-800x800-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="600">Samsung Galaxy S6 and S6 Edge</h2>
<p>There was certainly a lot to shout about when the Galaxy S6 and S6 Edge were released in 2015. Samsung&rsquo;s first &lsquo;Edge&rsquo; device gave a whole new mobile experience and certainly shook up the market.</p>
<p>With a more immersive borderless 5.1-inch screen, enhanced 16-megapixel main camera and new fast charging, 2015 was certainly a good year for Samsung with its Galaxy S6 range.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20588" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/s6-edge-gold-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="450"></p>
<p><span class="" style="font-size: 23px; color: #444444;">Next: See Galaxy S7/S7 Edge and Galaxy S8/S8 Plus.</span></p>
<p><!--nextpage--></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S7 and S7 Edge</h2>
<p>The following year, Samsung improved on its &lsquo;Edge&rsquo; handset again with the release of <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s7-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Galaxy S7</a> and S7 Edge with even more capabilities than before.</p>
<p>Galaxy S7 boasted a flat 5.1-inch HD display, dual 12-megapixel camera, enhanced performance for mobile gaming and of course, water and dust resistance was the feature of the time.</p>
<p>Sporting a larger 5.5-inch Quad HD screen, S7 Edge boasting gaming power, Samsung Pay and a larger 3600 mAh battery. Even in 2019, these devices still hold an impressive specification against newer rivals.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20580" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/samsung-galaxy-s7-sm-g930f-32gb-4g-black-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="600"></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S8 and S8 Plus</h2>
<p>In 2017, we saw the birth of the beautiful Infinity Display giving the Galaxy S8 range an edge-to-edge look. These devices also came with the new Samsung Bixby, your virtual assistant to help you with the day-to-day. Not only that, Dex (a handy mobile to desktop feature) launched too opening up new possibilities to Galaxy users.</p>
<p>In addition to top performance specs and detailed cameras, it has to be the design and display of the Galaxy S8 devices that truly made them stand out from the competition.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20590" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/s8-handset-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="444"></p>
<p><span class="" style="font-size: 23px; color: #444444;">Next: See Galaxy S9/S9 Plus, Galaxy S10/S10 Plus/S10e and Galaxy Fold.</span></p>
<p><!--nextpage--></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9 Plus</h2>
<p>With its Galaxy S9 range, Samsung took everything that was great about its S8 range and enhanced it, with a higher focus on phone photography.</p>
<p>The features that steal the show with the <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s9-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Galaxy S9</a> and S9 Plus are undoubtedly they're screen, camera and AR emoji feature.</p>
<p>With enhanced low-light capabilities and slow-mo, it&rsquo;s certainly and impressive setup. The S9 Plus variant sports a dual camera setup for even more intricate shooting. Watch, stream and game on an almost all-screen device and even turn yourself into an emoji with its AR Emoji, thanks to facial recognition mapping technology.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20589" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/s9-plus-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="484"></p>
<h2>Samsung Galaxy S10, S10 Plus, S10e and Galaxy Fold</h2>
<p>In 2019, Samsung decided to launch a trio of handsets, the <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10-128gb-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Galaxy S10</a>, <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10-plus-128gb-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">S10 Plus</a> and <a href="https://www.buymobiles.net/samsung/galaxy-s10e-black" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">S10e</a>.</p>
<p>Galaxy S10 takes uninterrupted viewing to the next level with its Infinity-O Display. Dubbed with &lsquo;A+ grade&rsquo; rated screen by DisplayMate (the phone tester people), it&rsquo;s safe to assume that this Galaxy screen is pretty next-level.</p>
<p>It&rsquo;s no surprise that Samsung upped its camera game with the S10 range. It has a triple lens camera with 12-, 12- and 16-megapixel to offer with each lens boasting a certain speciality, such as a ultra-wide lens, that all work in tandem together for amazing photos and video.</p>
<p>Samsung&rsquo;s One UI launched with the Galaxy S10 range, boasting a simple and clean design with a focus on night mode use and one-handed operation.</p>
<p>The Samsung Galaxy S10 range all come with an innovative Wireless PowerShare feature. This means your handset can charge your other smart tech with wireless charging capabilities, such as Galaxy Watch and other Samsung devices.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20575" src="https://storage.googleapis.com/a1comms-blog-buymobiles/1/2019/04/Galaxy-S10-family-edit.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="430"></p>
<p>Alongside its Galaxy flagships, Samsung had something else up its sleeve for us. The tech giant also announced a revolutionary &lsquo;foldable&rsquo; Galaxy device at its S10 launch event. With an expandable display, Galaxy Fold, powerful camera, user experience features, could the &lsquo;foldable&rsquo; phone concept really take off?</p>
