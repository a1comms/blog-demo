<p>An article about Instagram dog makeovers has a tenuous link to the mobile industry at best, but when you see just how heart-meltingly adorable these pooch before and afters are, the only question you&rsquo;ll want answering is where you can find more.</p>

<p>Chon confesses on her Instagram profile that she likes dogs better than people, and we can&rsquo;t find any reasons to disagree, after pawing through her feed of the most lovable dogs you&rsquo;ll ever happen upon.</p>
