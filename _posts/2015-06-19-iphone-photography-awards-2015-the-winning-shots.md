<p><span class="postStandFirst">IPPAWARDS 2015 winning shots: some haunting, some inspiring; all stunning.</span></p>
<p>The winners of this year&rsquo;s annual iPhone Photography Awards have been announced, bestowing an assortment of impossibly captivating images upon us all.</p>
<p>Since 2007, the IPPAWARDS has attracted the globe&rsquo;s greatest iPhone-wielding visionaries, to celebrate the seemingly boundless levels of their creativity in photography.</p>
<p>The 8<sup>th</sup> annual awards saw images submitted by shutterbugs from 120 countries, producing a smorgasbord of different subjects, landscapes and cultures.</p>
<p>The three winners of the Photographer of the Year award, as selected by the jury members, were Michal Koralewski of Poland, David Craik of the United Kingdom (yeah, go the UK&hellip;) and Yvonne Lu of the United States.</p>
<p>The 1<sup>st</sup>, 2<sup>nd</sup> and 3<sup>rd</sup> places in all of the other categories were awarded to a total of 57 photographers.</p>
<p>Each Photographer of the Year award winner is to be rewarded with an Apple Watch Sport, and those who emerged winners in the other categories are to look forward to a Gold Bar from the &lsquo;most recognisable private gold mint in the world.&rsquo;</p>
<p>Enjoy a selection of the winning shots below.</p>
