<p>If you own a Google Pixel, you&rsquo;ll want to hear about one of its coolest features (and if you don't own a Pixel, you'll want to by the end of this).</p>
<p>Introducing Playground, Google&rsquo;s Pixel camera mode that brings the world around you to life, with the help of Google lens, the tech company's augmented reality feature.</p>

<p>Google&rsquo;s Playground lets you beam your favourite characters, stickers or captions (otherwise known as Playmoji) into your photo or video. Pretty neat right!</p>
<p>Its latest pack features fan favourite singer/songwriter, Childish Gambino, busting some dance moves and singing some of his latest hits. Check it out in the video below:</p>

<p>The Childish Gambino Playmoji pack is available to download for free on Pixel. It's also available on the following devices: Google Pixel, Pixel XL, Pixel 2, Pixel 2 XL, Pixel 3 and Pixel 3 XL.</p>
<h2>What is Google Playground?</h2>
<p>Basically, we&rsquo;re looking at next-level AR stickers for your Google phone camera. The technology is a new mode that&rsquo;s been added to the Pixel camera, and can also be used in selfie mode, to help jazz up your selfie game.</p>
<p>It can even make suggestions of what to use based on your surroundings using its clever machine learning, recommending the right Playmoji sticker or effect for the moment.</p>
<p>The whole concept is designed around you being able to easily share on your social media too. Thanks, Google.</p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-20167" src="https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/NyBsDKAmUSbog4QEbtlw7UeszX_d8FRg-RA0HmJxVhbBCcGKXx9Y6GVlRw-fKCF9rRLPs9cQzDB2wn0_d_PJGGkF=s0" alt="" /></p>
<h2>What is Google Playmoji?</h2>
<p>Playmoji refers to the part of Google Playground packs that has AR characters, stickers or other featured icons.</p>
<p>The Playmoji react and <em>play</em> along with you in your selfie or video.</p>
<p>This means that you can now get some serious cameos into your images or videos from across a range Google packs.</p>
